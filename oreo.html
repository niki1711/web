Oreo Waffle Recipe

Here's how to make delicious Oreo Waffles:

  • In a mixing bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  • In another bowl, whisk together 1 cup of milk, 2 large eggs, and 1/4 cup of melted butter.
  • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  • Crush some Oreo cookies and fold them into the batter.
  • Preheat a waffle iron and lightly grease it with cooking spray.
  • Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions until golden brown and crispy.
  • Serve the Oreo waffles hot with additional crushed Oreos, whipped cream, and chocolate sauce for a delicious treat!
