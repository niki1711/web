Iced Coffee Recipe

Here's how to make a refreshing iced coffee:

  • Brew your favorite coffee using your preferred method (drip, French press, espresso, etc.).
  • Allow the brewed coffee to cool to room temperature.
  • Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  • Pour the cooled coffee over the ice cubes.
  • Add your desired amount of milk or cream, as well as any sweeteners or flavorings (sugar, flavored syrups, etc.).
  • Stir well to combine.
  • Optionally, top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon for extra flavor.
  • Insert a straw and enjoy your homemade iced coffee!
