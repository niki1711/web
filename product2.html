Chocolate Waffle Recipe

Here's how to make delicious Chocolate Waffles:

  • In a mixing bowl, sift together 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder, 1/4 cup of granulated sugar, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  • In another bowl, whisk together 1 1/4 cups of milk, 1/3 cup of vegetable oil, 2 large eggs, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  • Preheat a waffle iron and lightly grease it with cooking spray.
  • Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions until cooked through and crispy.
  • Serve the Chocolate Waffles hot with your favorite toppings such as fresh berries, whipped cream, or chocolate syrup.
