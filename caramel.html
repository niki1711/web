Salted Caramel Waffle Recipe

Here's how to make a delicious salted caramel waffle:

  • Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and a pinch of salt.
  • In another bowl, whisk together eggs, melted butter, milk, and vanilla extract.
  • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  • Cook the waffles in the preheated waffle iron until golden brown and crispy.
  • While the waffles are cooking, prepare the salted caramel sauce by melting butter in a saucepan, then adding brown sugar, heavy cream, and a pinch of salt. Cook until thickened.
  • Once the waffles are done, serve them hot with a drizzle of salted caramel sauce on top.
  • Enjoy your delicious salted caramel waffle!
