Classic Milkshake Recipe

Here's how to make a delicious classic milkshake:

  • Choose your favorite ice cream flavor such as vanilla, chocolate, or strawberry.
  • Add 2 to 3 large scoops of ice cream to a blender.
  • Pour in 1/2 to 1 cup of milk, depending on how thick you want your milkshake.
  • Optionally, add flavorings such as chocolate syrup, caramel sauce, or fruit.
  • Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  • Top with whipped cream and a cherry if desired.
  • Insert a straw and enjoy your classic milkshake!
