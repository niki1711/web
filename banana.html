Recipe

Here's how to make delicious vanilla ice cream:

  • Mix cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract in a bowl.
  • Churn the mixture in an ice cream maker until it reaches the desired consistency.
  • Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for several hours.
  • Scoop and serve with your favorite toppings!
