Fresh Fruit Smoothie Recipe

Here's how to make a delicious and nutritious Fresh Fruit Smoothie:

  • Choose a variety of fresh fruits such as bananas, berries, mangoes, pineapples, or peaches.
  • Wash and prepare the fruits by peeling, slicing, or chopping them into chunks.
  • Add the prepared fruits to a blender.
  • Optional: Add a handful of spinach or kale for added nutrients.
  • Pour in 1/2 to 1 cup of liquid, such as water, coconut water, almond milk, or orange juice, depending on your desired consistency.
  • Add a tablespoon of honey or maple syrup if you prefer a sweeter smoothie.
  • Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy.
  • Taste the smoothie and adjust sweetness or consistency as needed.
  • Pour the Fresh Fruit Smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.
  • You can garnish the smoothie with a sprinkle of chia seeds, coconut flakes, or a slice of fruit for presentation.
