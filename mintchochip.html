Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream Recipe

Here's how to make refreshing Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream:

  • In a mixing bowl, whisk together 2 cups of heavy cream, 1 cup of whole milk, 3/4 cup of granulated sugar, and 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract until the sugar is dissolved.
  • Add 1 teaspoon of mint extract and a few drops of green food coloring (optional) to the mixture and stir until combined.
  • Stir in 1 cup of chocolate chips or chopped chocolate.
  • Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  • Transfer the ice cream to a freezer-safe container, cover, and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  • Scoop into bowls or cones and enjoy the cool, minty goodness of homemade Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream!
