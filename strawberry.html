Strawberry Smoothie Recipe

Here's how to make a refreshing Strawberry Smoothie:

  • Combine 1 cup of fresh strawberries (hulled and sliced), 1 ripe banana (sliced), 1/2 cup of plain yogurt, 1/2 cup of milk (or a dairy-free alternative), and 1-2 tablespoons of honey or maple syrup in a blender.
  • Add a handful of ice cubes to the blender for a chilled smoothie.
  • Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy.
  • Taste the smoothie and adjust sweetness if necessary by adding more honey or maple syrup.
  • Pour the Strawberry Smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.
  • You can garnish the smoothie with a fresh strawberry slice or mint leaves for an extra touch.
